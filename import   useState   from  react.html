Lean Six Sigma

Teach, Assess, Adapt, Test, and Improve. A continuous cycle of educational quality.

Education Standards

We follow an integrated quality education standard as prescribed by European nations (IAEA).

Our methodology relies on the Lean Six Sigma principle:

  • Teach: Conceptual clarity.
  • Continuous Assessment: Regular homework tracking.
  • Adapt: Tailored to student intellect.
  • Test: Exams after every chapter.
  • Improve: Ongoing feedback to parents.

Assessment Strategy

Evaluation is based on homework completion and chapter-wise examinations. Parents are encouraged to assess independently and will receive timely updates.


Optimal Starting Age

Recommended for students aged 12-13 years (Grade 7, 8, or 9). No age limit for UK secondary examinations.

The Psychological Aspect

Our goal is to alleviate the academic burden. Students possess the ability to learn quickly; we condense 10 years of knowledge into manageable timelines.

This allows students ample free time in their formative years to enjoy life and promote healthy physical and mental growth.

Why Students Lose Interest

  • Overwhelming number of subjects.
  • Insufficient explanation of concepts.
  • Lack of adaptation to individual needs.
  • Fundamental errors in basic operations (addition, subtraction).

Our Solution: Streamlined curriculum, effective concept explanations, and teaching strategies that accommodate diverse learning styles.
